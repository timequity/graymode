# GrayMode Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 10, 2024

## Overview

GrayMode is a browser extension that applies grayscale filters to websites. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**GrayMode does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API.

## Data Stored Locally

The extension stores the following preferences on your device only:

- Enabled/disabled state
- Selected preset categories (Social Media, Video, News, Messengers)
- Custom blacklist of websites (up to 5 sites)
- Grayscale intensity level (50-100%)

This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Permissions Used

- **activeTab**: To apply the grayscale filter when you click the extension icon
- **storage**: To save your preferences locally on your device
- **host_permissions**: To apply the grayscale filter on websites you select

## Third-Party Services

GrayMode does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking.

## Remote Code

GrayMode does not execute any remote code. All functionality is contained within the extension package.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
